About BuzzGuard

BuzzGuard represents the future of indoor pest control, combining cutting-edge IoT technology with intelligent acoustic detection to create a seamless, automated solution for flying insect management.

Developed as a capstone project at Assumption College of Davao, our system addresses the persistent challenges of indoor flying insect control in tropical environments. Through sophisticated wingbeat frequency analysis and environmental monitoring, BuzzGuard provides real-time detection and automated repellent activation.

The Problems We Solve

Persistent Indoor Insect Infestation

Households continue to face problems with flying insects such as mosquitoes, flies, and gnats that spread diseases, contaminate food, and reduce comfort indoors.

Ineffectiveness of Traditional Methods

Conventional pest control tools like sprays, coils, and traps only provide short-term relief, require manual effort, and may expose users to harmful chemicals.

Lack of Smart, Continuous Monitoring

Existing control systems do not provide automated detection or real-time monitoring, leaving households without a sustainable or data-driven way to manage pest populations.

Our Solution

IoT-Based Automated Detection

BuzzGuard uses acoustic and environmental sensors to automatically detect the presence of flying insects based on sound frequency, temperature, and humidity.

Automatic Repellent Activation

Once insects are detected, the system triggers an essential-oil-based repellent through a smart diffuser, reducing insect activity without manual intervention or chemical sprays.

Mobile App Control and Alerts

A Bluetooth-connected mobile application allows users to monitor real-time insect activity, receive alerts, and control or adjust the system for optimal household protection.
